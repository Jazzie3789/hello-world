Choose Your Own Adventure

"Rise of the Dead"

Instructions

Welcome to Choose Your Own Adventure! You will be participating in an interactive story. All you have to do is refresh the page, if you haven't already. There will be a prompt box to show up and you can read through the story. At the end of most prompts will be choices for you to choose from. The choices you make will determine where you end up in the story, so choose wisely. You may reach a dead-end or you may find a solution. Keep playing and replaying to experience the many twists and turns!
