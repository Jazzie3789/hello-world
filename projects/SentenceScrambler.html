Instructions

Go to the top of your browser and click the "Customize and Control Google" option. From there, go to "More Tools". After you've clicked on "More Tools" click on "Developer Tools". This should open up your console window on the side of the page. For a shortcut you can simply press "Ctrl + Shift + I".

Now, type in the console: stringPrinter(). In between the parentheses type whatever phrase you want. After you've typed the phrase it should pop up on the page where these words are. Enjoy! For example, type: stringPrinter("Hello, my name is Computer) and then press ENTER.
